About Ashish General Store & Cafe

Since 2010, Ashish General Store & Cafe has been a beloved community hub owned by Pradeep Mahto and led by CEO Taru Devi. We combine everyday essentials with a cozy cafe and a modern cyber cafe experience. Our mission is to serve you quality products, delicious refreshments, and reliable internet access in a warm and welcoming environment.

Whether you’re picking up groceries, grabbing a snack, or connecting online, we’re here to make your day easier and better.
